Simple Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna comes with lots of pasta and cheese

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ham

Steps

  1. Heat the oil
  2. Add meat and water and boil
  3. Add tomato sauce and more water
  4. Make alternated pasta, cheese and ham
  5. Take to the oven with sauce until the cheese melt
