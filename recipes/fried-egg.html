Fried Egg

Description

This delicious egg comes with lots of olive oil so you can eat it with rice and banana!

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Whatever you imagine!

Steps

  1. On a frying pan add olive oil and butter
  2. Add the egg
  3. Add salt
  4. After a while, turn the ogg upside down
  5. Great, now eat
