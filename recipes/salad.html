Fried Egg

Description

There's nothing better to make your day better than a salad!

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Carrot
  • Beetroot
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Wash all ingredients
  2. Chop in small pieces
  3. Put it all together
  4. Make the sauce with sugar, salt, olive oil and vinegar
  5. Mix and that's it!
